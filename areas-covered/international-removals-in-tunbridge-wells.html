International Removals in Tunbridge Wells

If you are moving abroad and need a reliable removals company to ensure your move goes ahead smoothly, get in contact with Mark Ratcliffe Moving & Storage today!

How can we help you today?

Making Moving Abroad Easy

International Removals in Tunbridge Wells

Many people are choosing to move abroad due to an increasing range of opportunities and experiences abroad, and it is made easier by today's transport system being so accessible. Whether you are moving your business overseas, studying abroad or simply starting a new life somewhere new - we can help make sure your move goes ahead as smoothly as possible. We have many agents worldwide in over 200 countries who are all ready to help with your move. Your goods are closely tracked from the start of their journey, through customs and right up until they are safely unpacked at your new location. We are more than happy to set up all of your belongings so you can start your new life straight away.

We are made up of a fully qualified and insured team who have been in the industry for many years. We have the expertise to safely transport all of your belongings to your new destination, prioritising their safety. When you first enquire, we get to know your exact requirements and can adjust our service to suit your needs. The customer always comes first - we put your needs first so that you have a fantastic experience working with us. Over many years, we have grown into a renowned company with an outstanding reputation for providing reliable removals for international moves - just try us!

International & European Removals

We get to know your requirements and create a detailed plan as to which transport methods suit your move best. We have access to transport your goods via road, air and shipping - whichever may be most efficient. We even offer a packing service where our fully trained team will safely pack your belongings, only using the best tools and materials. If you prefer to pack your goods yourself, we recommend taking advantage of our high quality packing materials and tools which are available to be purchased at our shop. We offer various sized cartons, tissue paper, plain paper, bubble wrap and 2" vinyl tape. We are happy to unpack your goods at your new location if you require. We also offer portable wardrobe cartons so your clothes stay clean and crease free on their travels.

We can also help with commercial international moves if you are moving your business abroad. We provide A3 plastic stacking crates for files, dollies and trucks which cater for all offices. We can dismantle and reassemble any furniture you may require. We have many storage options available for all of our clients - we offer warehouse containers or storage rooms - whichever suits you. On depository, a full inventory will be taken of all of your goods. Then, you will be charged by the day so you only pay for the time you use the storage space. We work flexibly, 7 days a week and are available weekends and bank holidays should you require.
