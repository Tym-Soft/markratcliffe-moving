Assisting Your Home Move in Worthing

Professional House Removals in Worthing

Welcome to Mark Ratcliffe Moving  

Moving house in Worthing and struggling to choose a removals company? We understand how important it is to make the right choice. You need to know that you have secured good value for money and also be confident that the team arriving on the day will understand your requirements properly. They also need to have the correct training and equipment to ensure a straightforward and successful move.

Mark Ratcliffe Moving offer a complete moving service, handling your move from start to finish, for clients throughout Worthing, Brighton and across East and West Sussex. From moving single items like 3 piece suites across Worthing, to moving entire 4 bed households up to Scotland, we are well equipped with the latest equipment, knowledgeable staff and necessary skill to handle your move with ease.

Choose a moving company you can trust

Mark Ratcliffe has been working in the UK domestic removals and storage market for over 30 years. Working with customers throughout Worthing, the South East and nationally, quality has always been at the heart of everything we do. With a well respected reputation within the industry, our removal firm are one of just a handful with a dedicated staff training centre. All of our staff are fully covered and trained to the highest industry standards in packing fragile and non-fragile personal effects, wrapping and moving household furniture for home and overseas moving, as well as customer services and vehicle loading. You are guaranteed the highest standards, competitive prices and a genuine commitment from everyone involved in your move when choosing Mark Ratcliffe Moving.

Following your initial enquiry, Mark Ratcliffe will visit your home (at a time convenient for you) and carry out a site survey. During this time we can discuss all aspects of your forthcoming move including any packing requirements or insurance cover for your valuable possessions. A fixed quotation can be provided for moving, packing and storage and we also offer a house clearance service that we can provide further information on. On your moving day a fully trained and uniformed crew will arrive and lay down floor covers to protect your carpets, cover exposed doors and banisters etc. If required, they will also pack and move your belongings with the utmost care and transport them safely to your new home in Worthing or further afield.

Safe and secure storage 

Looking for storage in Worthing? As part of our comprehensive moving service, we offer secure self storage rooms in Worthing to rent for customers who prefer to deposit / remove their own goods to / from store. The rooms are available in various sizes with our most popular size being 77 sqft which equates to 850 cubic feet / 24 cubic metres. We rent this size room at £30 plus vat per week and with our storage solutions being much higher than local competitors and prices  being over 50% less, you receive great value for money. Please feel free to call in at any time to view our storage solutions and satisfy yourself that your possessions will be stored in a secure, damp free and clean environment. There is also great access for loading / unloading and you are the only key holder. 

Professional packing service for your home move in Worthing

Mark Ratcliffe Moving also offer a packing service including a full packing option and fragile item packing. If you decide to carry out your own packing, we can provide flat pack moving boxes, plain paper, bubble wrap, tape etc. All products are minimal in cost and they can be collected from our shop located in Eastbourne, 7 days a week.
