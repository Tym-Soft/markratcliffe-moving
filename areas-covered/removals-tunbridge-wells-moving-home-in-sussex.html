Premier Removals Tunbridge Wells and Kent

At Mark Ratcliffe Moving we offer a complete moving and storage service. Having worked in the industry for more than 30 years, you can put your trust in us to look after your move.

How can we help you today?

Moving House in Tunbridge Wells? Let us make your journey as 'Smooth as Silk'

Welcome to Mark Ratcliffe Moving Removals in Tunbridge Wells

When choosing the removals company for your moving day it is extremely important that you not only secure good value for money, but also be confident that the team arriving on the day will understand your requirements properly, having both the training and equipment necessary to ensure your move is seamless and stress free.

Mark Ratcliffe Moving offer a complete house removals or office removals service, handling your move from start to finish, for clients throughout Tunbridge Wells and across Kent. From moving single items like 3 piece suites locally, to moving entire 4 bed households up to Scotland, we are well equipped with the latest equipment, knowledgeable staff and necessary skill to handle your move with ease.

A removal company you can trust

Mark Ratcliffe has been working in the UK domestic removals and storage market for over 30 years. Working with customers throughout Tunbridge Wells, the South East and nationally, quality has always been at the heart of everything we do. With a well respected reputation within the industry, our removal firm are one of just a handful with a dedicated staff training centre. All of our staff are trained to the highest industry standards in packing fragile and non-fragile personal effects, wrapping and moving household furniture for home and overseas moving, as well as customer services and vehicle loading. You are guaranteed the highest standards, competitive prices and a genuine commitment from everyone involved in your move when choosing Mark Ratcliffe Moving.

Following your initial enquiry, Mark Ratcliffe will visit your home (at a time convenient for you) and carry out a site survey. During this time we can discuss all aspects of your forthcoming move including any packing requirements or insurance cover for your valuable possessions. A fixed quotation can be provided for moving, packing and storage and we also offer a house clearance service that we can provide further information on. On your moving day a fully trained and uniformed crew will arrive and lay down floor covers to protect your carpets, cover exposed doors and banisters etc. If required, they will also pack and move your belongings with the utmost care and transport them safely to your new home in Tunbridge Wells or further afield.

Safe and Secure Storage in Tunbridge Wells

Not only do we handle removals, storage is our specialty too. As part of our complete moving service, we offer secure self storage rooms in Tunbridge Wells to rent for customers who prefer to deposit / remove their own goods to / from store. The self storage rooms are available in various sizes with our most popular size being 77 sqft which equates to 850 cubic feet / 24 cubic metres. We rent this size room at £30 plus vat (per week) and with our storage rooms being much higher than local competitors and prices being over 50% less, you receive great value for money.

Please feel free to call in at any time to view our rooms and to see first-hand that your possessions will be stored in a secure, damp free and clean environment. There is also great access for loading / unloading and you are the only key holder. 

Professional Packing Service

Mark Ratcliffe Moving also offer a full packing service including a full packing option and fragile item packing. If you decide to carry out your own packing, we can provide flat pack moving boxes, plain paper, bubble wrap, tape etc. All products are minimal in cost and they can be collected from our shop located in Eastbourne, 7 days a week. 

Whether you are looking for office removals or house removals within Tunbridge Wells or further afield in the UK, or even abroad, Mark Ratcliffe Moving is ready to help. We can satisfy all of your wider moving needs including secure storage, house clearance and export packing. Our friendly team would be delighted to give you a quote without any obligation. Please call Sharon Harris on 01323 848 008 to talk through your moving requirements. Our opening hours are Mon - Sat 08.30 - 17.00 or we can arrange an appointment for any other time including Sundays. A 24/7 Service for our Self Store Customers is also available.
