How can we help you today?

Moving house in Seaford?

Moving home is one of life's most stressful events. There are so many things to remember and on top of that you have the task of trying to choose a reliable moving company. Securing good value for money, but also being confident that the team arriving on the morning of your move will be punctual, hardworking and equipped with the right training and equipment is vital.

Welcome to Mark Ratcliffe Moving 

Here at Mark Ratcliffe Moving we can satisfy all of these important requirements. Having worked in the UK domestic removals and storage market for over 30 years, we have extensive experience working with customers within the local area, across the South East and nationally. Mark Ratcliffe is well respected within the moving industry and has developed a reputation for a quality service that competitors strive to achieve.

With a dedicated staff training centre, our staff are trained to the highest industry standards in packing fragile and non-fragile personal effects, wrapping and moving household furniture for home and overseas moving, as well as customer services and vehicle loading. The highest standards, competitive prices and a genuine commitment from everyone involved in your move is guaranteed when choosing Mark Ratcliffe Moving.

Our Moving Service

Unlike other removal companies, we offer a complete moving experience that takes care of every aspect of your move from start to finish. Whether you have a single piece of furniture that needs moving across town, or a 4 bedroom home whose contents need relocating up to Scotland - big or small, we can handle it all.

Following your initial enquiry, Mark Ratcliffe will visit your home (at a time convenient for you) to carry out a site survey. During this time we can discuss all aspects of your forthcoming move including any packing requirements or insurance cover for your valuable possessions. A fixed quotation can be provided for moving, storage and packing and we also offer a house clearance service that can be discussed if required. On your moving day a fully trained and uniformed crew will arrive and lay down floor covers to protect your carpets, cover exposed doors and banisters etc. If requested, they will also pack and move your belongings with the utmost care and transport them safely to your new property.

Packing  

Packing is time consuming and when you are moving home time is not always something you have a lot of. That is why we offer a full packing service, whether you require complete packing or simply some of the more fragile items to be specially packed, our team are trained in packing and will ensure your possessions are well protected. If you decide to carry out your own packing, we can also provide flat pack moving boxes, plain paper, bubble wrap, tape etc. All products are minimal in cost and they can be collected from our shop 

Self Storage Units  

We offer secure self storage rooms to rent for customers who prefer to deposit and remove their own goods. The self storage units are available in various sizes with our most popular size is the 77 sqft which equates to 850 cubic feet / 24 cubic metres. This room is charged at £30 plus vat (per week) and with our storage rooms being much higher than local competitors and prices being over 50% less, you receive great value for money.

The area of Seaford

Seaford is a stunning costal town located on the south cost of England in the county of East Sussex. With the surrounding areas of Newhaven and Brighton, Seaford has a population of appproximately 27,000. With a quaint pebble beach, Martello Tower Museum, Crypt Art Gallery, a vibrant town centre, there are many things to do and see.
