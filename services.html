Our Removals Services in Sussex

Get a Free Quote Call 01323 848 008

Mark Ratcliffe Moving & Storage offers a complete range of removals services across Eastbourne, Sussex, the UK and overseas. From a single piece of furniture to a four-bedroom international relocation, every job we take on is handled by our directly-employed crews — uniformed, BAR-trained and using the same pad-wrap method that has kept our customers’ furniture intact for over forty years. Use this page as your map to every service we run, then click through to the detail page that fits your move.

Local and regional removals

The core of our business is home removals across East and West Sussex. Whether you’re moving across Eastbourne or out to Brighton, Hastings, Worthing or Horsham, you’ll get the same survey-quoted, fixed-price service. Two-person crews for flats and small terraces, three- and four-person crews for family homes, and we routinely cover the longer routes out to Surrey, Kent and London on a single load day.

Removals Eastbourne

Our home town. Local crews, local depot, local knowledge of the steep streets and the seafront parking suspensions.

Man & Van

Smaller loads, single rooms, partial moves and one-off pieces — uniformed crew, proper materials, fixed price.

Removals Brighton

From Hove terraces to Kemp Town flats — we know the parking-permit zones and the hill streets that need a smaller van.

Removals Hastings

Old Town twittens and the East Hill — we routinely shuttle a smaller van where the lorry cannot fit.

Removals Worthing

Bungalow downsizing and seafront flats. Most Worthing moves are completed within a single day.

All areas covered

The full list of towns and villages across Sussex, Surrey and Kent that we serve as standard.

Packing, materials and unpack

Packing is the single biggest determinant of whether things arrive intact. We offer four ways to handle it: full pack, fragile-only, materials supplied to you, or buy materials by the box. Add the unpack-on-arrival option and you can be living from your new home the day we leave.

Packing Services

Our pad-wrap method — the standard our BAR-trained packers use on every job.

Full Packing

We pack absolutely everything. Two-day pack for a 3-bed home is typical.

Unpacking Service

Live from your new home the day we leave. Boxes flat-packed, cardboard removed.

Buy Packing Materials

Removal-grade boxes, tape, bubble and blankets — order by email for collect or delivery.

Specialist and high-value moves

Some items need more than a strong back and a pad-wrap. For pianos, antiques, art, designer interiors and the kind of household where insurance values run to six figures, we offer a specialist track. Different crew, different van fit-out, different paperwork.

Piano Moving

Upright, baby grand, full grand. Skid trolleys, climate-aware vans, proper insurance.

Antiques Moving

Climate-stable transit, archival tissue, bespoke crating where the piece warrants it.

White-Glove Service

Designer furniture and high-end interiors — uniformed crew, full inventory, condition report.

Custom Crating

ISPM-15 timber crates for international shipment of paintings, sculptures and clocks.

Commercial, student and house clearance

Not every move is a family relocation. We run dedicated services for offices that need to move outside trading hours, for students moving in and out of halls each term, and for executors clearing a property in probate. Each has its own logistics and price model.

Office Removals

Out-of-hours moves, labelled-and-mapped desks, IT-aware handling.

Student Removals

Hall-to-hall, hall-to-home, multi-stop — small loads at student-friendly prices.

House Clearance

Probate-aware, charity-routed where possible, certified disposal documentation.

International, European and self storage

We’ve been moving households overseas since the early 1990s and are members of the British Association of Removers Overseas Group, the Furniture Industry Ombudsman International section and the IMA. For everything outside your moving day we run our own steel-strong-room self storage at the Lower Dicker depot.

International Removals

Worldwide door-to-door with our overseas-group partners. Full export packing.

European Removals

Weekly groupage services to France, Spain, Portugal, Italy, Germany and the Low Countries.

UK – Thai Removals

Specialist UK-to-Thailand service with our own Bangkok partner depot.

Self Storage

Prestige steel strong rooms at Lower Dicker. Daily access, climate-stable.

Frequently asked questions

How do I get a quote?

Email office@markratcliffemoving.co.uk, call 01323 848 008, or use the online quote form. For homes larger than two bedrooms we always recommend a free survey — we’ll come and walk through the property and email you a fixed-price quote within 24 hours.

Are you insured?

Yes. We hold full goods-in-transit, public liability and professional indemnity cover, plus optional all-risks transit insurance that customers can elect on top. Full insurance details are on our Terms & Insurance page.

Do you do same-day moves?

Often yes, depending on the size and our crew availability. Call us — do not email — if you need a same-day or next-day move and we’ll tell you straight away what we can do.

Do you supply boxes?

Yes. Materials are included in every full removal we quote. If you’re packing yourself, you can buy removal-grade materials from our packing materials page.
