How services usually combine on a real Sussex move

Most customers don’t book just one service in isolation. The common combinations follow the move’s shape. A typical local Eastbourne move combines the man-and-van service (for a smaller home) or full house removals (for a 3-bed+) with packing materials and, often, unpacking at the new property. A long-distance move adds storage if the chain timing isn’t perfect. An overseas move adds international shipping, custom crates for anything fragile, and our partner-agent destination service in the destination country.

The order to book services in

Confirm your move date first, even provisionally. Then book the removal — that’s the longest-lead-time service and the one that anchors everything else. Once that’s in the diary, decide on packing (full-service or self-pack with materials), unpacking, storage if needed, and any specialist items (piano, antiques, art) that need separate handling. The 20% deposit covers the removal itself; packing and unpacking can be added later without affecting the removal booking.

When a single service is enough

For small jobs — a single item, a student move, a delivery from auction — the man-and-van service is usually all you need. No survey required, hourly billing, two-person crew available within a week. For these jobs there’s no benefit to adding packing or unpacking services; you’re paying for the lift and the lorry, nothing more.

Frequently asked about Our Removals Services in Sussex

How do I get a quote?

Email office@markratcliffemoving.co.uk, call 01323 848 008, or use the online quote form. For homes larger than two bedrooms we always recommend a free survey — we’ll come and walk through the property and email you a fixed-price quote within 24 hours.

Are you insured?

Yes. We hold full goods-in-transit, public liability and professional indemnity cover, plus optional all-risks transit insurance that customers can elect on top. Full insurance details are on our Terms & Insurance page.

Do you do same-day moves?

Often yes, depending on the size and our crew availability. Call us — do not email — if you need a same-day or next-day move and we’ll tell you straight away what we can do.

Do you supply boxes?

Yes. Materials are included in every full removal we quote. If you’re packing yourself, you can buy removal-grade materials from our packing materials page.
