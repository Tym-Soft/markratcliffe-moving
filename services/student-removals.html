Student Removals Across Sussex

Get a Free Quote Call 01323 848 008

If you’re a student moving in or out of halls at the University of Sussex, the University of Brighton, the University of Chichester or any of the Sussex colleges, our student removals service is built around the realities of your timetable: short notice, tight budgets, narrow stairwells in halls and a moving day that doubles as exam-finish day. We offer fixed-price small-load moves between halls, halls and home, or to and from storage — with a uniformed two-person crew, proper packing materials and a single price quoted up front.

What our student removals service covers

We move undergraduates, postgraduates and international students between any combination of:

  • University halls of residence (Falmer, Brighton, Lewes, Chichester, Eastbourne)
  • Private student accommodation and HMOs
  • Family home anywhere in the UK
  • Our own self storage depot at Lower Dicker (perfect for summer storage between academic years)
  • Airports for international students returning home

Typical loads range from a single room (one car-load equivalent) up to a four-person shared house. We bring all the materials — cartons, bubble wrap, tape, blankets — and you only pay for what you use.

Hall-to-hall, hall-to-home, hall-to-storage

The three most common student moves we handle:

  1. Hall-to-hall. Moving rooms or buildings between terms. We work around hall handover deadlines and parking restrictions on campus.
  2. Hall-to-home. End of year, you’re going back to mum and dad. We collect from your room, drive directly to home and unload. One day, one price.
  3. Hall-to-storage. Going travelling for the summer? We collect from your hall, label and inventory your gear, store it in our secure unit at Lower Dicker (from £15/week for a typical single-room load) and re-deliver to wherever you need it in September.

Pricing for student moves

Honest prices for student-sized loads:

  • Single hall room → home within Sussex: typically £180–£280 inclusive of materials, mileage and crew.
  • Single hall room → home elsewhere in UK: £250–£450 depending on distance.
  • Shared house (3–4 students) → various destinations: £350–£650 for a half-day with two crew.
  • Summer storage: from £15/week including insurance.

We quote a fixed price up front. No mileage surcharges, no end-of-day “the lorry overran” charges, no £25 stair fees. The price you’re quoted is the price you pay.

September and June — book early

Our two busiest weeks of the year are the second week of September (start of term) and the third week of June (end of term). Diaries fill 6–8 weeks ahead in those windows. If you know your move date, get in touch as soon as you do — we’ll hold a slot and confirm details closer to the date.

Mid-term moves (e.g. moving rooms in March, or moving into a new flatshare for the second year) we can usually accommodate at a few days’ notice.

What you get with every student move

  • Uniformed, directly-employed crew (no casual labour).
  • Proper materials — not bin bags. Boxes, bubble wrap, tape, mattress bag, blanket for the desk and chair.
  • Goods-in-transit insurance included to the value of your inventory.
  • Fixed price, quoted before we arrive.
  • Card or bank transfer payment — no cash needed on the day.
  • A real human you can call if anything changes — we’re a Sussex family business, not a national franchise.

Local universities and colleges we cover

We regularly run student moves to and from:

  • University of Sussex — Falmer campus (East Slope, Lewes Court, Park Village, Northfield, Stanmer Court)
  • University of Brighton — Moulsecoomb, Falmer, Eastbourne and Hastings campuses
  • University of Chichester — Bishop Otter and Bognor campuses
  • Sussex Coast College Hastings
  • East Sussex College Eastbourne
  • Plumpton College
  • Private student blocks: Mithras House, Hove Park, Phoenix Halls, Sussex Heights, Iona House, and the various Unite, Vita and Student Castle blocks across the region.

International students returning home

For international students, we run a combined storage + onward shipping service: store your gear with us over the summer, then we pack, document and ship anything that’s going home with you when you graduate. We’re BAR Overseas members and ship regularly to most of Europe, the United States, Canada, Australia, India, China and South-East Asia. Email office@markratcliffemoving.co.uk with your final destination and approximate volume and we’ll come back with a shipping quote.

Frequently asked questions

How do I book a student move?

Email office@markratcliffemoving.co.uk with your collection and delivery addresses, your move date and a rough idea of what’s coming (one room? full shared house?). We’ll reply with a fixed quote the same working day. For September and June we recommend booking at least 6 weeks ahead.

Can you do same-day or next-day student moves?

Often yes outside the September and June peak weeks. Call rather than email if you need it that quickly: 01323 848 008.

Do I have to pack everything before you arrive?

No. Most student customers pack themselves to save money, but if you’d rather we packed for you, fragile-only or full packing is an add-on. Tell us when you book.

How does summer storage work?

We collect from your halls or flat, drive everything back to our secure depot at Lower Dicker, store it on a per-week basis from £15, and re-deliver to wherever you need it. Goods-in-transit and storage insurance included.

Do you take cards?

Yes. Card payment over the phone or bank transfer. Some students pay half on booking and half on delivery — we’re happy with that.

My parents want to pay &mdash; can you invoice them?

Of course. We routinely invoice parents, family members, employers (for postgrad relocations) and university accommodation offices. Just give us the billing address when you book.
