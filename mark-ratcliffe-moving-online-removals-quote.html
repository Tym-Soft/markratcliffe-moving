Why request your quote from Mark Ratcliffe Moving?

We’re a family-run Sussex removals company that’s been moving households across East Sussex, West Sussex, Surrey and Kent since 1982 — over forty years of practical experience our crew brings to every quote. Every customer deposit is protected by the BAR Advance Payment Guarantee under our British Association of Removers membership, and our processes are certified to BS 8564, the international removals standard. We’ve packed crystal, wrapped mahogany, manoeuvred grand pianos and arranged container shipping to Thailand — and our written quote will reflect exactly what your move actually needs, with nothing hidden.

If you prefer to talk it through, call us on 01323 848 008 and one of the team will pick up. We respond to every online quote within one working day.
