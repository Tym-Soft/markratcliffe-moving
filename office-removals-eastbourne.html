Our commercial moving service

Pre-move planning and site survey

Every office move starts with a site survey at both the origin and the destination. We map floor plans, count workstations, identify IT cabinets and server rooms, check lift access and parking restrictions, and discuss the timing window. The output is a project plan — typically a Gantt-style schedule from a few hours' work for a small office through to a phased move across multiple weekends for larger sites.

IT equipment handling

IT is where most office moves go wrong. We label every monitor, every PC, every cable bundle with its destination floor, room and desk number. Sensitive equipment is wrapped in anti-static bubble. Servers travel last and arrive first, in a dedicated van. We can coordinate with your IT team or your managed-service provider to schedule the disconnect-reconnect — or, for smaller offices, we can recommend a local IT partner.

Furniture dismantling and reassembly

Most modern office furniture — desks, partitions, storage cabinets, meeting-room tables — needs to be dismantled to fit through lifts and doorways. Our crews include experienced furniture fitters. We bring the right tools, we keep every fixing in labelled bags, and we reassemble at the destination.

Out-of-hours moves

The most common request: "we close at 6pm Friday, we open at 8am Monday — can you do the move in between?" Yes. Friday-evening / Saturday moves are our most common office pattern. Bank-holiday Mondays work well for longer moves.

Secure document handling

Paper records are a particular liability under UK GDPR. We can transport sealed file boxes between offices, transfer them into our Prestige steel storage for long-term retention, or arrange GDPR-compliant secure destruction via our partner with a certificate of destruction issued for each batch.

Project management — your single point of contact

Office moves involve a lot of moving parts: your facilities team, your IT team, the building manager at both ends, the landlord's representative, sometimes a fit-out contractor. We assign a single project lead from our team who takes on the coordination, runs the pre-move site visits, attends planning calls, and is on-site through the move itself. You deal with one person, not a different remover each time.

Pricing for office moves

Office removal pricing is based on volume of equipment, distance between premises, complexity of dismantle / reassemble, IT scope, and downtime tolerance (out-of-hours and bank-holiday work attracts a premium). We do not publish flat per-desk rates because it leads to under-quoting and bad outcomes for customers. Every office quote follows a site survey and is itemised — you see exactly what you are paying for.

A typical Eastbourne office move — phased over three nights

To give a concrete sense of how we work, here is the schedule for a recent 40-person professional services office move in Eastbourne. The customer wanted no operational downtime — staff in the old office Monday through Wednesday, in the new office Thursday onwards.

Monday evening, 6pm–10pm. Pre-move site visit and final inventory. Floor protection laid at both ends. Crates delivered to each desk for staff to pack their own desk contents the next day.

Wednesday evening, 6pm–midnight. Heavy furniture, filing cabinets and meeting-room contents moved. IT cabinets disconnected, labelled and transported (server rack last to leave, first to arrive). Crew of six plus IT team.

Thursday morning, 5am–8am. Final workstations arrive at the new office. IT team reconnects. Cleaners do a final pass. Staff arrive at 8.30am to fully operational desks.

Total operational downtime: zero. Total weekend disruption: zero. Total cost: roughly £4,800 for the moving element (plus IT consultancy and cleaning charged separately).

The hidden costs of a badly planned office move

Office moves go wrong for predictable reasons. Most of them have nothing to do with the physical moving of furniture — they are about the things that get forgotten in the planning phase. Here is what we see most often:

  • Underestimated downtime. A move that "should take a weekend" actually takes three days of disrupted productivity if IT is not properly coordinated. Plan a contingency Tuesday.
  • Missed building rules. Many commercial buildings restrict move-times to specific windows (often out of hours), require dedicated lifts to be booked, demand insurance certificates from the remover, and charge for floor-protection installation. Confirm the rules at both ends weeks in advance.
  • Missing IT inventories. If nobody has a list of what equipment is in which corner of the office, things get lost. Photograph everything before the move and keep a master inventory.
  • Forgotten contracts. Phone lines, broadband, water coolers, photocopier leases — every one has a separate change-of-address process. Start them six weeks ahead.
  • Cabling chaos. Cables not labelled before disconnect mean hours of IT trial-and-error after re-plug. Photograph the back of every workstation before unplugging.

Our pre-move planning catches each of these before they become a problem.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can you move our office out-of-hours?

Yes — evening, weekend and bank-holiday moves are a standard option. Many of our office customers move on a Friday evening / Saturday so they can start trading on Monday in the new premises.

How do you handle our IT equipment?

Each workstation is labelled in advance with its destination floor, room and desk. We use anti-static bubble wrap for sensitive items and we transport in dedicated, climate-controlled compartments. We can also liaise with your IT team to coordinate disconnect and reconnect.

Are you insured for high-value office equipment?

Yes. We carry full goods-in-transit insurance, and additional declared-value insurance is available for high-value equipment (servers, lab equipment, art collections). We will quote based on your declared inventory value.

Can you store office records during a fit-out?

Yes — short and long-term document storage at our Prestige steel facility. We can also arrange GDPR-compliant secure destruction of old paper records via our partner.
