Removals in Bognor Regis — Coastal & Bungalow Specialists

Get a Free Quote Call 01323 848 008

Bognor Regis is one of our easier patches — flat streets, generous parking, lots of bungalows, plenty of esplanade flats with lifts, and an A259 corridor that runs straight east to our Eastbourne depot. About 50% of our Bognor work is bungalow downsizing for customers retiring to the coast, another 25% is seafront-flat moves, and the rest is a mix of family-home relocations between Bognor, Aldwick and Felpham. Mark Ratcliffe Moving covers PO21 and PO22 with experienced regional crews who do this route weekly.

Areas of Bognor Regis we cover

  • Bognor Regis town centre — Belmont Street, High Street, Waterloo Square.
  • The esplanade — Esplanade, Marine Drive, Lennox Street. Lots of retirement flats with lifts.
  • Aldwick & Aldwick Bay — west of Bognor, family-home and downsizer territory.
  • Felpham & Middleton-on-Sea — east of Bognor, mix of bungalows and modern estates.
  • Pagham & Nyetimber — quieter coastal villages, bungalow-heavy.
  • North Bersted & South Bersted — inland Bognor suburbs, family-home estates.
  • Yapton & Barnham — villages north of Bognor on the way to Arundel.

Why Bognor downsizer moves work so well

Bognor and the surrounding coast have one of the highest bungalow densities in the country. Combined with the strong London-and-Home-Counties retirement migration, that makes downsizer moves the single biggest category of our Bognor work. The typical Bognor downsizer move looks like this:

  • Survey at both ends. The old family home (often a 3- or 4-bed in Surrey or West London) and the new Bognor bungalow.
  • Inventory-driven decluttering. Most downsizers can’t take everything. We help identify what fits.
  • Full pack 1–2 days before move day. You don’t lift a box.
  • Move day. Lorry from the old home, drive direct to Bognor, unload.
  • Unpack on arrival. Kitchen filled, bed made, wardrobe hung. Live from your new home that night.
  • Charity routing. Surplus items routed to your chosen charity that week.

Common Bognor move types

Surrey/London ↔ Bognor retirement

The classic Bognor move. Often a 4-bed Home Counties family home into a 2-bed coastal bungalow.

Esplanade flat moves

Marine Drive and Esplanade retirement flats. Most have lifts. Our crew brings ramps.

Bungalow-to-bungalow within Bognor

Felpham seaside cottages

The seaside-cottage end of the market. Quiet streets, easy parking.

Why people choose us for Bognor removals

Three things to look at when choosing a Sussex remover:

  • BAR membership. The British Association of Removers audits members annually and runs an Advance Payment Guarantee for deposits. We’re full BAR members. Many cheaper operators are not.
  • Directly-employed crew. Not casual day labour. Our team train at our own training centre — one of only a handful of UK movers that has one.
  • Pad-wrap as standard. Every piece of furniture wrapped in heavy quilted blankets before it leaves your room — not in the lorry. This is the single biggest reason for our damage-free record over 40 years.

Frequently asked questions

Do you specialise in downsizer moves?

It’s our biggest Bognor service category. Combined pack + move + unpack + charity-routing in a single quote — we’ve refined the process over decades.

Do you cover PO21 and PO22?

Yes — full Bognor Regis postcode area plus Aldwick, Felpham, Middleton-on-Sea, Pagham, Nyetimber, North and South Bersted, Yapton and Barnham.

Can you store contents between two move dates?

Yes — at our Lower Dicker depot. Climate-stable, individually-roomed, accessible by appointment. Common when the new property completion date slips.

How early should I book a Bognor move?

4–8 weeks for standard moves. End-of-month dates fill earlier — book at the start of the chain process if you can.
