Not every move needs a 7.5-tonne lorry and a full pre-pack. How man-and-van differs from a full removal — and which is right for you

Customers sometimes ask whether man-and-van is a "cheaper version" of a full removal. The truthful answer is no — it is a different service for a different job. A full removal is a fixed-price, planned, surveyed move with packing, dedicated lorry capacity and a structured day. Man-and-van is an hourly-billed, smaller-scale service for jobs where a surveyed quote would be overkill. For a 1-bedroom flat move within Eastbourne, man-and-van is almost always cheaper and entirely appropriate. For a 3-bedroom house move, man-and-van billed hourly typically ends up more expensive (and more stressful) than a properly quoted full removal — because the hours add up faster than people expect.

How to keep your man-and-van booking efficient

Because the service is billed hourly, the things you can do before the crew arrives translate directly into pounds saved:

  • Be packed before the van arrives. Everything in boxes, taped shut, stacked near the front door. The crew should not be waiting for you to pack.
  • Pre-dismantle awkward furniture. Beds, flat-pack wardrobes and large IKEA pieces are faster if you have already dismantled them. Keep the fixings in labelled bags.
  • Park-clear at both ends. If parking right outside is uncertain, move your own car earlier to keep the space.
  • Have a destination plan. Know which room each item is going to. Tell the crew on arrival — saves "where do you want this?" trips.

Frequently Asked Questions

How much is your man and van service in Eastbourne?

Our hourly rate starts from £55 per hour for one mover plus driver, with a 2-hour minimum. A weekend or evening surcharge applies. Larger vans and additional movers are quoted separately.

Can you do same-day man and van in Eastbourne?

Sometimes — it depends on our diary. We do not promise same-day in advance; call us and we will tell you honestly what we can fit in today.

What is the difference between man-and-van and a full removal?

Man-and-van is hourly, uses a smaller vehicle (Luton or 3.5-tonne), suits small flats, single items and student moves. A full removal is quoted as a fixed job, uses larger lorries and includes our full pad-wrap protection and packing service.

Do you supply boxes for man-and-van jobs?

Yes — we can drop materials in advance or you can collect from our Lower Dicker depot. Pad-wrap and blankets are included with the vehicle.

Can I use man-and-van for an IKEA collection?

Absolutely — IKEA flat-pack collections, eBay pickups and Facebook Marketplace runs are some of our most common man-and-van jobs. Tell us the address and dimensions and we will quote.
