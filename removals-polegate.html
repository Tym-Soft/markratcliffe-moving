Why local matters when you choose a Polegate removal company

Choosing a local removal firm for your Polegate move is not just about convenience — it is about cost, reliability and routing knowledge. From our Lower Dicker depot, our crews reach Polegate via the A22, A27 and A2270, and they know the area's quirks: where parking is tight, which streets have low bridges, where the parking-suspension applications need to go and what time the school-run traffic builds up. National movers pricing in a Polegate job have to factor in the long empty leg back to their yard; we do not, so our quotes are typically more competitive for Polegate addresses.

We are also part of the local community. You will recognise our vans on the A22, A27 and A2270, you may have seen them at Polegate Railway Station, the Pony pub and the Old Mill, and our family has been part of Sussex life for over forty years. Many of our customers are repeat clients or referrals from neighbours we moved years earlier.

We cover Polegate town centre, Willingdon Trees, Wannock, Filching and Folkington as part of our standard Polegate service area, with no extra travel charges within these districts.

How we protect your possessions

Every Polegate move uses our signature pad-wrap process. Each piece of furniture is individually wrapped in a thick quilted blanket inside your home, taped, labelled, then carried out — and only unwrapped once it is placed in its final position in your new property. This is the same method used by high-end international movers, and it is why our breakage rate is a fraction of the industry average.

What sets us apart from generic national movers

Generic national removal firms quote your move based on volume and postcode distance — but they cover everywhere, so they know nowhere. They will not know the parking restrictions on your street, the tight stairwell in the older terraces, or the school-run window that determines whether a 7.5-tonne lorry can manoeuvre at 8.30am. We do. We are also accountable locally — many of our customers are repeat clients or referrals from neighbours, so the move-day experience matters to us in a way that a national chain simply cannot replicate. And finally, on price: our quotes typically beat the nationals on local moves because we are not pricing in the empty leg back to a Midlands depot.

What to expect on move day with us

Our crews follow the same routine on every move, regardless of property size: arrive 8.00–8.30am, walk the rooms with you, lay floor protection, pad-wrap furniture in the room before carrying out, load in a planned sequence, walk through the empty house with you before locking up, head to the new address, unload in reverse order placing furniture in the right room, unwrap and place each item, beds reassembled, final walk-through, sign-off. The whole process for a typical 3-bedroom local move runs 8am to 3-4pm.

How long does a typical Polegate house move take?

A 2 or 3-bedroom local move from Polegate usually completes within a single day. A 4 or 5-bedroom or longer-distance move may need two days. We will tell you honestly during the survey.
