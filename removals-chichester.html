Removals in Chichester — Cathedral City Specialists

Get a Free Quote Call 01323 848 008

Chichester is one of our most distinctive removals patches. The cathedral city has hundreds of Grade II listed properties with narrow doors, the surrounding villages have country homes with significant antique contents, the Witterings have a coastal cottage character all of their own, and Goodwood’s events calendar dictates which weekends a removal van can actually get into the area. Mark Ratcliffe Moving has been working PO19 and the surrounding West Sussex postcodes since the mid-1980s, with crews who know listed-property handling, antique-furniture protocols and the Goodwood Festival of Speed traffic restrictions.

Areas of Chichester we cover

Chichester city, the surrounding villages and the Manhood Peninsula:

  • Central Chichester — East, West, North and South Streets, the Pallants, Little London, St Pancras. Listed properties, narrow doors, permit parking.
  • Summersdale & Whyke — northern suburbs, family-home territory.
  • Fishbourne & Bosham — west of the city, harbour-side. Lots of period and listed-cottage moves.
  • Lavant, East Lavant, Mid Lavant — villages north of Chichester. Country homes with long drives.
  • Goodwood & Boxgrove — the Goodwood estate cottages, plus the village of Boxgrove with its priory.
  • The Witterings — East Wittering, West Wittering, Birdham. Coastal cottages, second-homes, family bungalows.
  • Selsey & the Manhood Peninsula — bungalow downsizer territory, retired-people pockets.
  • Tangmere & Eastergate — east of Chichester, family-home estates.

Listed-property removals — what changes

Chichester has more Grade II listed properties per square mile than almost any other Sussex city. Moving in or out of one isn’t fundamentally different from any other move, but a few things matter:

  • Door clearances. Many Georgian and earlier doors are smaller than modern doors. We measure at the survey — a sideboard that fits a 1990s house may not fit a 1740s townhouse.
  • Stairs. Eighteenth-century stairs are usually narrower and steeper than modern ones. The turn at the bottom of the stairs is often the bottleneck.
  • Original floors. Period oak boards mark easily. We lay full floor protection from front door through every working room.
  • No drilling, no adjustments. Listed-building consent prohibits screwing into the structure to anchor a hoist. We use freestanding lifting equipment only.
  • Council notification. Some city-centre listed properties require a council notification before commercial vehicles park outside. We handle that for you.

Common Chichester move types

Cathedral city listed-property moves

Georgian and earlier townhouses. Slow, careful, full floor protection.

The Witterings cottage moves

Often second-home owners moving in or selling on. Tight lanes, beach parking restrictions.

Goodwood estate cottages

Estate-owned properties around the racecourse and the house. Often involves coordination with the estate office.

Antique-heavy country homes

The villages north of Chichester have a lot of antique furniture. See antiques service.

Chichester ↔ London relocations

The Chichester-to-London corridor is well-used by retirees. We run this route monthly.

Selsey bungalow downsizing

Selsey is a downsizer hub. Bungalow-to-bungalow moves are routine.

Goodwood weekends — the diary problem

Four weekends a year (Festival of Speed late June/early July, Qatar Goodwood Festival late July/early August, Revival mid-September, plus the Members’ Meeting in March/April), the area around Goodwood becomes effectively closed to removal lorries. Traffic restrictions, parking suspensions, and the sheer volume of event traffic mean we don’t take Chichester removals on those dates. The Goodwood events calendar is published 12+ months ahead — we cross-reference every Chichester quote against it before confirming.

Why people choose us for Chichester removals

Three things to look at when choosing a Sussex remover:

  • BAR membership. The British Association of Removers audits members annually and runs an Advance Payment Guarantee for deposits. We’re full BAR members. Many cheaper operators are not.
  • Directly-employed crew. Not casual day labour. Our team train at our own training centre — one of only a handful of UK movers that has one.
  • Pad-wrap as standard. Every piece of furniture wrapped in heavy quilted blankets before it leaves your room — not in the lorry. This is the single biggest reason for our damage-free record over 40 years.

Frequently asked questions

Have you done listed-property removals before?

Yes — dozens in Chichester alone, plus the wider Sussex Grade II and Grade II* market. Full floor protection, freestanding lifting equipment, and we’ll coordinate with your conservation officer if needed.

Do you cover PO19 and PO20?

Yes — full Chichester city and the surrounding West Sussex postcodes including the Witterings, Selsey, Bosham, Fishbourne, Lavant and the Manhood Peninsula.

What about the Goodwood event weekends?

We don’t schedule Chichester moves on Festival of Speed, Qatar Goodwood Festival, Revival or Members’ Meeting weekends — access is too restricted. We cross-check every quote against the Goodwood calendar 12+ months ahead.

Can you handle antique furniture from a Chichester period property?

Yes — our antiques moving service is regularly paired with Chichester moves. Acid-free materials, cotton-glove handling, optional all-risks insurance.

How far ahead should I book a Chichester move?

4–8 weeks for standard moves. Longer if you’re moving across one of the Goodwood weekends — in which case we’ll need to book around it.
