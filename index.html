Frequently Asked Questions

How far in advance should I book my Eastbourne removal?

We recommend booking 4–6 weeks ahead during peak Sussex moving season (May to September). For off-peak moves we can often accommodate 1–2 weeks' notice. A 20% deposit locks in your guaranteed move date.

Do you cover Newhaven, Lewes and the rest of East Sussex?

Yes. From our Eastbourne and A22 Lower Dicker bases we cover the whole of East Sussex including Newhaven, Lewes, Seaford, Hastings and Bexhill, plus West Sussex (Brighton, Worthing, Hove) and Surrey (Guildford, Reigate, Woking).

What if my move date changes?

There is no charge for postponements, cancellations or key waits. We simply update the diary at no extra cost — one of the things our Sussex customers tell us they value most.

Do you offer secure storage between moves?

Yes — our Lower Dicker site offers individual steel Prestige self-storage rooms with 24-hour CCTV and alarmed access. Ideal for short-term storage between Sussex moves or longer-term household storage.

Can you handle international removals from Sussex?

Absolutely. We are a founder member of Your Moving Group with vetted partner agents in over 200 countries. See our overseas removals page for more on international moves from Sussex.
