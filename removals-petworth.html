Removals in Petworth — Trusted Movers Since 1982

Get a Free Quote Call 01323 848 008

Petworth is the antiques capital of southern England — a market town with dozens of antiques dealers, the National-Trust Petworth House on its western edge, and a town centre that’s almost entirely conservation area. Many of the homes in and around Petworth contain significant antique furniture, which makes the antique-handling element of our work here unusually important. We pair every Petworth move with our specialist antiques protocols.

Areas of Petworth we cover

  • Petworth town centre — Market Square, Lombard Street, High Street
  • Petworth House area — estate cottages
  • Tillington & Egdean — villages east
  • Byworth & Coultershaw — villages south
  • Kirdford & Plaistow — country villages north

Antique-furniture handling

Petworth properties often contain genuinely valuable antiques. We use archival tissue, cotton-glove handling, custom timber crates where individual pieces warrant it, and we elect all-risks transit cover at declared value as standard for Petworth moves.

Why people choose us for Petworth removals

Three things to look at when choosing a removals company:

  • BAR membership. The British Association of Removers audits members annually and runs an Advance Payment Guarantee for deposits. We’re full BAR members.
  • Directly-employed crew. Not casual day labour. Our team train at our own training centre.
  • Pad-wrap as standard. Every piece of furniture wrapped in heavy quilted blankets before it leaves your room — not in the lorry. The single biggest reason for our damage-free record over 40 years.
