About Mark Ratcliffe Moving & Storage

Four decades of moving Sussex families — from a single Luton van in Hampden Park to a purpose-built A22 depot with self-storage, secure strong-room and a modern UK and international fleet.

Our story

Four decades of moving Sussex

Mark Ratcliffe’s career in the Sussex removals industry began on 1st January 1982 with a single second-hand Luton van and a small shop on Hampden Park, Eastbourne. Forty-four years on, three companies later, the principles haven’t changed: a personal survey, a fixed quote, pad-wrapped furniture, and a uniformed crew who turn up when they say they will.

1982 — A.1. Removals

Mark founds A.1. Removals with one Luton van and a second-hand shop in Hampden Park, Eastbourne. By the mid-90s the firm is the largest independent mover in Sussex — 28 staff, 11 trucks, four trailers and two Eastbourne warehouses totalling 26,000 sq ft.

2014 — Mark Ratcliffe Moving

After selling A.1. Worldwide Movers in 2006 and The Small Moving Company in 2014, Mark founds his third and current company. The promise: “Good old-fashioned service” at an honest price — whether the job is a single item across town or a four-bedroom country move abroad.

2020 — Lower Dicker depot

The company moves to a purpose-built warehouse on the A22 at Lower Dicker, just north of Hailsham. A secure mezzanine strong-room serves moves-in-storage; the ground floor is a fully fitted self-storage facility with 24/7 digital door entry.

Today

The Lower Dicker depot

Our headquarters on the A22 at Lower Dicker is the largest single removals depot in this part of East Sussex. The mezzanine floor is fitted as a secure strong-room for goods we hold in our care; the ground floor is a fully kitted self-storage facility with individual digital-entry doors and 24-hour access.

The depot lets us hold loads overnight if a chain slips, store furniture between sales, and run separate domestic, commercial and international shipments without the bottlenecks a smaller yard would create. It also means our crews start and finish from the same site every day — lorries serviced on-site, blankets laundered in-house, no agency labour cycled through.

Mark Ratcliffe Moving also operates a Worldwide service for small baggage shipments and complete household moves to over 200 countries and territories. Every overseas job is overseen personally — nothing is sub-contracted to a third-party broker.

What sets us apart

Six reasons families choose us

BAR member since the 1990s

Registered with the British Association of Removers and bound by their Code of Practice — meaning deposits are protected under the Advance Payment Guarantee.

Family-run since 1982

Three Mark Ratcliffe companies, one consistent ethos. Survey by a director, crew that has worked here for years, and the phone answered by name.

Pad-wrap as standard

Every freestanding piece of furniture is wrapped in heavy quilted blankets before it leaves the room — included on every full removal, not a premium add-on.

Fixed-price quotes

A 30–45 minute in-home or video survey, then a written, itemised quote with the price locked. No move-day surcharges if the inventory is what we surveyed.

Our own depot & storage

Loads can be held overnight, between sales or for longer periods at the A22 Lower Dicker site. No outsourced storage, no third-party handovers.

BS 8564 accredited

Independently audited to the British Standard for moving services, FHIO accredited for international shipping, and members of the UK–Thai Movers Group.
